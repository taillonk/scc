Audience Information
Audiences Issues,Concerns,Needs Approach
Fans: These are younger people; men and women in their twenties. Passionate about their favorite artist. They are always online since they mainly use a smartphone, and therefore, often on a mobile network. They prefer using a music streaming app, following their favorite artist on social media, streaming their music, and attending concerts.
  • A website that is easy to navigate and looks good on mobile and desktop.
  • A website that contains links to music videos, songs, and streaming platforms.
  • A website that contains information about concerts.
  • A website where they can purchase merchandise.
  • A website that offers opportunities to interact with the artist; links to social media or newsletters with exclusive content.
  • The website will incorporate a simple layout, but it will be appealing and responsive.
  • The site will include a page for music with links to the artist's streaming platforms and a section for music videos.
  • The site will include a page with information about concerts with links to buy tickets.
  • The site will include a page for merchandise.
  • The site will include a page with links to the artist's social media plarforms.
  • The site will include an option for fans to sign up to newsletters.
Record Label Rep: These are music industry professionals. Men and women in their 30s and up, always on the go. Computer savvy. They use a high-end laptop with a high-resolution screen and a fast internet connection.
  • A website that is easy to navigate and looks good on mobile and desktop.
  • A website that contains links to music videos, songs, and streaming platforms.
  • A website with a bio page where the record label can get to know the artist.
  • A website that is reflective of the artist's brand.
  • A website that offers opportunities to get in touch with the artist; contact page and links to social media.
  • The website will incorporate a simple layout, but it will be appealing and responsive.
  • The site will include a page for music with links to the artist's streaming platforms and a section for music videos.
  • The site will include a detailed biography of the artist, including his background, influences, and journey.
  • The site will include color scheme, images and visuals that represent the artist's work and brand.
  • The site will include a page with links to the artist's social media plarforms.
  • The site will include a contact page for inquiries.
Talent Managers: These are music industry veterans, always connected. Men and women in their 30's and up. Computer savvy. They use a tablet, smartphone, or a high-end laptop with a high-resolution screen and a fast internet connection.
  • A website that is easy to navigate and looks good on mobile and desktop.
  • A website that contains links to music videos, songs, and streaming platforms.
  • A website with a bio page where the record label can get to know the artist.
  • A website that is reflective of the artist's brand.
  • A website that offers opportunities to get in touch with the artist; contact page and links to social media.
  • The website will incorporate a simple layout, but it will be appealing and responsive.
